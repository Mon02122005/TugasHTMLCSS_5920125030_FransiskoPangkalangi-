The , or berger de brie,is a large breed of dog traditionally used as a herder and guardian of sheep...

The briard, which is believed to have originated in Frence, has been bred for centuries to herder and to protect sheep. The breed was use by the Frence Army as sentries, messengers and to search for wounded soldiers because of its fine sence of hearing.
