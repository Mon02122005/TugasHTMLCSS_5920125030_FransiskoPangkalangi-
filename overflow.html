Fender Stratocaster

The Fender Stratocaster or "Strat" is one of the most popular electric guitars of all time, and its design has been copied by many guitar makers. It was designed by Leo Fender,George Fullerton and Fredie Tawares in 1954

Gibson Les Paul

The Gibson Les Paul is a solid body electric guitar that was first sold in 1952. The Les Paul was designed by Ted McCarty in collabaration with popular guitarist Less Paul, whom Gibson enlisted to endorse the new model. it is one the most well known electric guitar types in the word.
